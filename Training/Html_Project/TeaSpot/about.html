About Us

Serving Since 1950

Our Story

In India, tea is not just a drink. It's part of our heritage and culture. Tea has been grown in the country for over 150 years. India is one of the world's leading tea producers. India is the second-largest producer and supplier of teas in the world. India has spent more than 90% on imported tea. The majority of the quality tea gets exported to other countries for packaging and then sold back in India as imported goods in the Indian market.

"Develop effective management strategies to facilitate: Competence and innovation in tea plantations, Innovative processing technology for producing good quality teas, Augmentation of high value tea exports, Capacity Building for human resources at all levels in tea Industry, Strengthening of R & D efforts on all aspects of tea husbandry and technology."

Our Vision

Our goal is to provide the best quality tea experience for our customers. Truly Teas wants to continue the trend of providing healthy beverage options and by combining drinking excellent teas with their daily lifestyle. We want to change the way people see and think about tea, one cup at a time.

To make India, the leading producer and supplier of quality tea in the global market.

"Our tea website not only lists tea businesses from tea plantations to tea shops and tea rooms, but also features world tea news, articles and even press releases. Our news and articles section will be of interest to a tea expert, tea sommelier and everyone in the tea industry."
